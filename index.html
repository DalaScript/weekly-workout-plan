Progress Rules

Simple rules to keep progress safe and consistent.

Rule 1 — Use RPE 7/10

Finish most sets with about 2–3 reps left in the tank. Do not train to failure in the beginning.

Rule 2 — Add Weight Slowly

If you complete all sets at the top of the rep range, increase the weight slightly next time.

Rule 3 — Track Every Workout

Write down exercise, weight, reps, how it felt, and any pain or discomfort.

Rule 4 — Deload Every 4th Week

Reduce weights by about 20–30% and focus on clean movement.

4-Week Progression

Repeat, improve, then recover.

Week 1Learn technique, keep everything light
Week 2Repeat exercises, improve control
Week 3Add small weight or reps where possible
Week 4Deload: reduce weight and focus on clean movement
